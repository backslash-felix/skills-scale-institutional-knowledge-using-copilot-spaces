# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX / Design Lead

### Role Summary
Owns the UX vision, design systems, and usability validation. Bridges user research and engineering to ensure the product is intuitive and accessible.

### Responsibilities
- Define and maintain design standards and component libraries
- Conduct and synthesize user research and usability testing
- Collaborate with Product Managers on problem framing and solution design
- Review PRs and acceptance criteria for UX impact
- Advocate for accessibility and inclusive design

### Goals
- Deliver consistent, high-quality user experiences
- Reduce usability issues discovered post-release
- Ensure design decisions are grounded in user evidence

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and prototype walkthroughs
- Collaboration with PdM on feature specs and user stories
- Feedback in PR reviews and sprint demos

---

## Tech Lead / Engineering Lead

### Role Summary
Provides technical direction and architectural oversight for the delivery team. Partners closely with Project and Product Managers to balance velocity with quality and maintainability.

### Responsibilities
- Own technical architecture decisions and design reviews
- Set and enforce engineering standards (code quality, testing, security)
- Identify and mitigate technical risks and dependencies
- Mentor developers and support technical onboarding
- Coordinate with cross-team engineers on shared systems

### Goals
- Deliver scalable, maintainable solutions
- Minimize technical debt while meeting delivery commitments
- Ensure observability and reliability of deployed systems

### Typical Communication
- Technical design docs and architecture reviews
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- Cross-team dependency coordination

---

## Security & Compliance Officer

### Role Summary
Ensures that projects meet security, privacy, and regulatory requirements. Engaged early in planning and throughout execution to prevent compliance gaps.

### Responsibilities
- Review project scope and architecture for security risks
- Define security acceptance criteria and compliance requirements
- Participate in threat modelling and risk assessments
- Oversee security scanning and penetration testing
- Lead security incident response and post-incident reviews

### Goals
- Embed security into the SDLC rather than bolt it on at release
- Reduce security vulnerabilities and compliance violations
- Maintain audit-ready documentation

### Typical Communication
- Security review sign-offs during planning and pre-release
- Incident communication and post-mortems
- Regular updates to the Risk Register

---

## Scrum Master / Delivery Facilitator

### Role Summary
Facilitates agile ceremonies, removes impediments, and coaches the team on continuous improvement. Works alongside the Project Manager to keep delivery flowing.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate sprint planning, standups, reviews, and retrospectives
- Identify and remove blockers and process inefficiencies
- Track team velocity, capacity, and health metrics
- Coach team members on agile practices and collaboration
- Shield the team from unplanned interruptions

### Goals
- Maintain a sustainable, predictable delivery cadence
- Build a self-organizing, high-performing team
- Continuously improve team processes through retrospectives

### Typical Communication
- Sprint ceremony facilitation
- Blocker escalation to PM or Product Lead
- Team health and velocity reports

---

## Stakeholder / Executive Sponsor

### Role Summary
Provides strategic direction, funding authority, and final escalation point for the project. Champions the initiative at the organizational level.

### Responsibilities
- Approve project initiation, scope changes, and major decisions
- Remove organizational blockers that the team cannot resolve
- Provide strategic context and business priority guidance
- Review and sign off on key milestones and releases
- Represent the project to senior leadership

### Goals
- Ensure the project delivers intended business outcomes
- Maintain organizational alignment and support
- Make timely decisions to prevent delivery delays

### Typical Communication
- Monthly stakeholder updates and milestone reviews
- Escalation decisions as needed
- Executive briefings and roadmap presentations

---

## Support / Customer Success Representative

### Role Summary
Represents the voice of the customer throughout the project lifecycle. Provides feedback from support channels and ensures release communications reach customers effectively.

### Responsibilities
- Surface customer feedback, pain points, and feature requests
- Review release notes and customer-facing documentation
- Coordinate support readiness and training before releases
- Monitor post-release customer sentiment and issue trends
- Escalate critical customer issues to the PM and Product Lead

### Goals
- Minimize customer-impacting issues at release
- Ensure customers are informed and supported through changes
- Close the feedback loop between customers and the delivery team

### Typical Communication
- Pre-release readiness reviews
- Release notes and customer communications
- Post-release feedback summaries and escalations
